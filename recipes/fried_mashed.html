Fried Mashed Potato Cakes

Description

Fried mashed potatoes in the form of patties. Yummy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup leftover mashed potatoes, cooled
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix mashed potatoes, egg, flour, and salt in a bowl until well combined.

  2. Heat a thin layer of vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat.

  3. Working in batches, drop 1/6 of the potato mixture into the hot frying pan and flatten into a patty. Repeat to add more patties to the pan. Cook until browned, about 3 minutes per side.
